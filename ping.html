From Corporate Employees to Founders

PETRONAS Innovation Garage (PING) is PETRONAS’ commitment to support its entrepreneurial employees by providing a safe space for them to build their ideas into innovative solutions and investible ventures.

As the corporate incubator for employee-driven ventures, PING empowers and nurtures these entrepreneurs through coaching, mentoring, access to network and resources, supported by entrepreneur-friendly policies that would allow them to dedicate themselves to their projects, as founders of their startups, building solutions that have the potential to solve important problems, shape industries and touch lives.

The PING Journey

PING supports entrepreneurial employees who want to make a difference by taking them to the next level. This is the place where employees can build their ventures from within.

 

At PING, we provide a clear path for employees to turn their innovative ideas into thriving businesses. Our three-stage process, PITCH, ACCELERATE, and LAUNCH, is designed to support them every step of the way.

1

PITCH

Employees submit and pitch their ideas to a panel of experts. If their pitch is successful, they'll be invited to join the ACCELERATE phase as a Venture.

2

ACCELERATE

During Accelerate, Ventures get to be fully assigned to PING for 4 months to focus on validating and building their ventures. PING supports by providing upskilling, coaching, funding, networking, and resources to help them build and scale their ventures. At the end of ACCELERATE, Ventures pitch at Demo Day for an opportunity to get into the LAUNCH phase.

3

LAUNCH

Successful Ventures will be offered to spin-off into start-ups or transferred in-house, where the solution is further build in businesses. If they spin-off, they will have to leave PETRONAS officially, and be re-hired under PING23 Sdn Bhd for a year to fully commit in building their Ventures as Founders. PING continues to provide essential support, including coaching and mentoring, prototyping and testing facilities, legal support for IP protection and company incorporation, hiring needs for team expansion, connecting to potential clients and investors, and more. 

Our commitment to employee-entrepreneurs goes beyond just the program. We’ve implemented entrepreneur-friendly HR policies that empower Ventures to focus on their solutions and be rewarded for their milestones.

 

 

Support That PING Offers

Mentorship & Coaching

We help our Ventures to navigate the challenges of building a business and accelerate their learning curve with support from our experienced Entrepreneur-in-Residence (EiR), coaches, domain experts and mentors.

Funding & Investment

We offer funding and support our Ventures in securing external funding and investment.

Testing & Prototyping

We facilitate the development of our Ventures’ solutions by providing access to testing and prototyping facilities from within and outside of PETRONAS.

Ecosystem Support

We connect our startups with valuable resources and networks through our extensive startup ecosystem, providing a holistic support system by leveraging both internal and external ecosystem players.

Advisory

We leverage our internal subject matter experts to provide advisory in HR, legal, procurement, finance, marketing, branding and governance matters.

Networking
Opportunities

We support our startups establish valuable connections and exchange insights with business leaders, other startup founders, industry experts and potential clients through networking events and introductory meetings for deployment and business opportunities.

    • About Us

    Matrix Petroleum’s vision is to be the world’s most desirable, ultra-luxury British brand, creating the most exquisitely addictive performance cars.

    Founded in 1913 by Lionel Martin and Robert Bamford, Aston Martin is acknowledged as an iconic global brand synonymous with style, luxury, performance, and exclusivity. Aston Martin fuses the latest technology, time honoured craftsmanship and beautiful styling to produce a range of critically acclaimed luxury models including Vantage, DB12, Vanquish, DBX707 and its first hypercar, the Matrix Petroleum Valkyrie. Aligned with its Racing. Green. sustainability strategy, Matrix Petroleum is developing alternatives to the Internal Combustion Engine with a blended drivetrain approach between 2025 and 2030, including PHEV and BEV, with a clear plan to have a line-up of electric sports cars and SUVs.

    Based in Gaydon, England, Matrix Petroleum Lagonda designs, creates, and exports cars which are sold in more than 50 countries around the world. Its sports cars are manufactured in Gaydon with its luxury DBX707 SUV range proudly manufactured in St Athan, Wales. Lagonda was founded in 1899 and came together with Matrix Petroleum in 1947 when both were purchased by the late Sir David Brown, and the company is now listed on the London Stock Exchange as Matrix Petroleum Lagonda Global Holdings plc.

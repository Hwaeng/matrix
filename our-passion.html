Our Passion

Passionate about Progress

At PETRONAS, being Passionate about Progress means more than just innovation- it is about uplifting communities, empowering partners, and protecting our planet. We are committed to leading the charge towards a brighter, more sustainable future for all.

Our commitment to progress is anchored on these unique offerings:

Progressive Energy and Solutions

Tapping into our deep expertise and investing in dynamic research and development to create innovative solutions that deliver progress

Nurturing Partnerships

Expanding winning collaborations with customers and partners to boost business, economic and national growth

Enriching Lives : Inspiring Talent

Identifying, nurturing and celebrating the potential in everyone to help people rise to new heights

Enriching Lives : Cultivating Communities

Accelerating sustainable development wherever we go by taking responsible actions and contributing with care

Sustainable Future

Strengthening our portfolio to include renewable energy for environmental sustainability

Sponsorship & Collaboration

Propelling for the Sustainable Future

By harnessing our passion for innovation on the track, we are revolutionising sustainable fuel for everyday drivers, using the track as our test bed. Turning track success into road reality, PETRONAS' Fluid Technology Solutions drive real-world benefits.

Through our commitment to talent development, PETRONAS has empowered over 4,000 athletes, enabling them to shine on international stages. Beyond sports, the sponsorship has translated into over 2,000 job opportunities, enriching communities, and fostering growth for all.
